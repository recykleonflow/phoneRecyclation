1
Prepare Your Phone

Before you recycle your old phone, follow these steps to protect your personal data and ensure a smooth recycling process

  • Back up your data
  • Perform a factory reset
  • Remove your SIM card and any external storage devices
  • Charge your phone's battery to at least 50% (if possible)
2
Find Drop Off Point

Once you have prepared your phone, visit the nearest recycling drop-off point.
Personnel will take care of the rest, ensuring that your device is responsibly recycled and its materials are reused in an environmentally friendly manner.

3
Spread the Word

Encourage others to recycle their old phones by sharing our app with friends, family, and colleagues.
Together, we can make a difference and contribute to a greener future.

Why is Recycling Important?

Recycling your old phone helps to reduce waste, save energy, and conserve natural resources.
It also prevents hazardous materials from entering the environment and supports the recycling industry, creating jobs and promoting a sustainable future.
